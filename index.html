Game Features

  • competitive play - race against a friend!
  • multiple race rooms - experience our diverse yet consistently structured set of race rooms.
  • speed boosting - boost your running speed to out pace your opponent
  • randomly generated race tracks - one of our races is generated on the fly as players navigate from platform to platform. Youll only know where to go next once your there!
  • checkpoints - this is a race to the end, your avatar cannot die for good, but will instead respawn at the most recently passed checkpoint when killed.
  • rotating platforms - watch the colour of the platforms, if they turn red they are about to flip!
  • traps - maneuver your avatar over,around or through the series of traps placed throughout the races.

How to play:

You begin in the Homeroom, choose the racetrack you wish to enter by walking through its door, this will transport you into the chosen Elbowroom. Once in an Elbowroom (race track), touch the white start line which will start the countdown to begin the race. Once the race has begun, do your best to out maneuver your opponent, dodge the traps, and avoid falling off platforms to be first to reach the back door of the Elbowroom, the end of the race!
