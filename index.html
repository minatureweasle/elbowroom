Game Description

Elbowroom. There isn't much space to party. But a monkey can try. Run, jump and fall towards victory in an epic race of small proportions. Where you begin? You begin in a room, surrounded by THINGS (stay calm)! Your friend will be there, and he probably wants to race you. Amongst the clutter, you see a door. Where does this door lead you don't know, but you and your friend are getting inpatient. You approach this door. Wabaaam, zip zap swing, you are in a whole different world. You are in an Elbowroom.
